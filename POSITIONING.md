# Where2Meet.Me — Positioning

## One-liner

Find great spots to meet your people.

## Tagline

**Meet in the middle, at a place you'll love.**

## Value Prop

Drop your locations, pick a vibe, and we'll find great places that are easy for everyone to get to. Real travel times, real places — done in seconds.

## Key Messages

1. **Great places, not just coordinates.** We surface top-rated venues — cafes, bars, restaurants, parks — not a pin on a map.
2. **Easy for everyone.** Nobody gets stuck with a 45-minute trek while someone else walks five. We use real travel times to keep it convenient.
3. **Done in seconds.** Drop your locations, tap go. No signup, no fuss.
4. **Share and meet.** One link sends everyone the spot, with directions built in.

## Tone

Warm, confident, slightly playful. Think Citymapper meets Google Maps meets a friend who knows great spots. We're the friend who says "I know a place" — and actually does.

## Words to Avoid (user-facing copy)

- fairness, equality, parity
- mathematical, optimal, algorithm
- midpoint (keep in code/internal only)
- balanced (when it sounds clinical)

## Words to Lean Into

- great, easy, everyone, meet, spot
- quick, convenient, perfect (colloquial)
- your group, your people
- love, enjoy

## Score Presentation

Instead of "XX% Match" with clinical labels, use:

| Score     | Label              | Vibe                          |
|-----------|--------------------|-------------------------------|
| ≥ 95%     | Perfect for everyone | Chef's kiss — can't do better |
| ≥ 85%     | Great for everyone | Very convenient all round     |
| ≥ 70%     | Good for everyone  | Solid pick                    |
| < 70%     | Decent pick        | Worth considering             |

## Section Rename: "Travel Parity" → "Travel times"

Show travel times transparently — people understand "12 min vs 18 min" intuitively. No need to call it "parity."
